The only known planet with Life.

Earth, our home planet, stands out as the only known celestial body to harbor life. Its unique combination of liquid water, an oxygen-rich atmosphere, and protection from the Sun’s harmful radiation makes it a haven for diverse and persistent lifeforms, from microscopic organisms to complex ecosystems.

ertha or erda.

The name Earth has roots in various languages. In Old English and Germanic, it simply means “the ground” or “soil”. Unlike other planets, Earth’s name lacks connections to Greek or Roman mythology, emphasizing its down-to-earth origin.
